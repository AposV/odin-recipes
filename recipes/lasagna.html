Nonna's Lasagna

Follow this quick and easy recipe which will reward you with mouthwatering lasgna, just how Nonna used to make them!

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to make the Lasagna Step-By-Step

Here's a brief overview of what you can expext when you make homemade Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sausce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasgna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasgna rest before serving
